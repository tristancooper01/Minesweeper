Left click to reveal squares, Right click to flag or chord.



Tutorial: The grid is hidden, some squares have mines . Your goal is to reveal all squares except for squares with mines. If you reveal a mine, you lose. Revealed squares tell you how many mines are in the 8 adjacent squares. You can place a flag on a square you know has a mine, which stops you from accidentally clicking on it. If you place a flag on a square with no mine, and then lose, this will show that your flag was misplaced.
